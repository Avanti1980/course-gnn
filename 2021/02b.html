特征变换 核 PCA

$$ \begin{align*} \text{核 PCA}: ~ \max_{\|\wv\|_2^2 = 1} \| \Phi(\Xv) \wv \|_2^2, \quad \Phi(\Xv) = \begin{bmatrix} \phi(\xv_1)^\top \\ \vdots \\ \phi(\xv_m)^\top \end{bmatrix} \end{align*} $$

记$\Kv = \begin{bmatrix} \kappa (\xv_1, \xv_1) & \cdots & \kappa (\xv_1, \xv_m) \\ \vdots & \ddots & \vdots \\ \kappa (\xv_m, \xv_1) & \cdots & \kappa (\xv_m, \xv_m) \end{bmatrix}$为核矩阵，核 PCA 可重写为

$$ \begin{align*} \text{核 PCA}: ~ \max_\alphav ~ \alphav^\top \Kv \Kv \alphav \quad \st ~ \alphav^\top \Kv \alphav = 1 \end{align*} $$

通过拉格朗日乘子法求得系数$\alphav$后，样本$\xv_j$在成分$\wv$上的投影为

$$ \begin{align*} \langle \wv, \phi(\xv_j) \rangle = \sum_{i \in [m]} \alpha_i \kappa (\xv_i, \xv_j) \end{align*} $$
